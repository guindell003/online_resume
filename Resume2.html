Vulnerability Management

Posted on 14 May by Nic Weilbacher

I joined the Vulnerability management team when Nationwide first started building out their security capabilities. I am responsible for the majority of the processes and procedures for how the team operates today.
The Vulnerability management umbrella consists of 2 parts: Vulnerability Scanning, and Vulnerability Response.

Vulnerability Scanning focuses on scanning our network connected assets for vulnerabilities; while Vulnerability Response is designed to mitigate critical vulnerabilities in a timely fashion working with our Infrastructure support teams to maintain a high level of security and compliance.

My notable accomplishments(VS/VR):

  • (VS)Weekly scanning of the entire network using Qualys
  • (VS)All vulnerability data is imported into a Netezza DB
  • (VR)Trained new employees and developed their security skills
  • (VS/VR)Created a collaboration framework between applicable teams within SCC to respond to and mitigate emerging threats efficiently
  • (VS)Collaborated with Threat Intel team to automate detection and response processes for emerging threats
  • (VS)Developed multiple automation scripts to gather data from tools for analysis and VR process improvements
